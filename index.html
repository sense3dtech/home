GAMING MARKET AND OPPORTUNITY

The Total Addressable Market (TAM) for the video game industry is projected to reach an astonishing 197 billion dollars in the year 2022. This rapid growth is not limited to traditional gaming platforms but extends to the mobile gaming sector as well. The Serviceable Available Market (SAM) has experienced an impressive increase from 1.7 billion in 2019 to a remarkable 3 billion by 2022, reflecting a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) surpassing 8%. In the context of India, the impact is equally notable, with an astounding 507 million players, showcasing the remarkable reach of the Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) and establishing India as a global leader in the gaming industry.

Opportunity in Gamification

Gamification has the potential to transform the landscape of school education. With approximately 33.2% of the global population, equivalent to around 2.6 billion individuals, falling under the age of 20, the scope for innovative learning experiences is immense. This demographic expanse is further segmented, with approximately 1.3 billion individuals being under the age of 14 globally. In India alone, a remarkable 25.68% of the population, roughly 350 million individuals, comprises those under the age of 14. These statistics underscore the vast educational landscape and the significant potential gamification holds in shaping the future of learning.
